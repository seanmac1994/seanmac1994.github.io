MEMBERSHIP PRICES

Joining Elm Green has many perks and our price for a membership is second to none.
Our membership includes priority tee off times, full access to our club house, 1000 free balls at the driving range, free pitch and putt play and 2 free lessons with a choice of 3 of our PGA professionals.

The price of our 1 year membership is €750.

DRINK

In our clubhouse we have a bar that is open from 10 a.m until late.
We do not encourage drinking whilst playing golf, but we allow it, once it is done in moderation.
Our bar sells a wide range of beers including imported beers from around the world.

FOOD

The clubhouse also has a food menu, that sells homemade food at the ready. Meals like homemade stew, chicken curry, fish and chips.
Or if your just in the mood of a light snack you can try our special club sandwhich.

Members get a 25% discount on all food and drink.

DEALS AND DISCOUNTS

If you join with Elm Green you will be gifted with a numerous amount of deals and discounts.
10% off at the golf shop
Free pitch and putt
50% off Driving Range balls
25% off food and drink

For members who want to come back after their year membership runs out, you get 10% off next years membership.
This discount will stack each year!
